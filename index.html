Privacy Policy – Sugar Lens

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Sugar Lens allows users to manually enter and store the following information:

  • Personal information (such as name or profile details, if provided by the user)
  • Health-related information (including age, weight, blood glucose readings, meal logs, and medication schedules)

All information is entered voluntarily by the user.

2. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on the user's device.

Sugar Lens does not upload or store personal or health data on external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Sugar Lens may connect to the USDA FoodData Central API to retrieve nutritional information about foods identified by the user.

Only the detected food name is used in the request. No personal or health data is transmitted.

4. No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share user data for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Medical Disclaimer

Sugar Lens provides informational health insights only and is not a medical device.

The app does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions.

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your doctor or local emergency services immediately.

6. Your Control

  • You have full control over your information.
  • Deleting the app will remove all stored data.

7. Nutritional and Medical Sources

Sugar Lens uses publicly available information from reputable sources:

    https://fdc.nal.usda.gov
